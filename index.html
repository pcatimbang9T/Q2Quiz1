Leron, Leron Sinta originated in the Tagalog Region Leron, Leron Sinta is usually sang by farmers during work. It is represented as a children's song, however the in-depth lyrics are for a mature audience
Nasaan Ka, Irog? by Nicanor Abelardo Nasaan ka Irog is a song that talks about love and courtship. The Term "irog" means lover. It is a real life song wherein Nicanor Abelardo expresses his frustration and helplessness, due to broken love.
Magtanim Ay 'di Biro by Felipe de León The song sings about the struggles of farmers in the Philippines who plant rice in the muddy paddies all day.
Paru Parong Bukid by Felipe De Leon Paru-parong Bukid is a humorous song about comparing a Filipino lady in her glamorous formal gown with tall butterfly sleeves to that of a field butterfly, like a capricious woman who walks around to attract attention.
Lupang Hinirang by Julián Felipe The Philippine National Anthem, an expression of ideals for our people and symbolization of the struggles and bloody sacrifices the Filipino people had to endure.
